Why Tiger Woods is The G.O.A.T.
  1. Acheivements
    • He was the top-ranked golfer in the world from August 1999 to September 2004 (264 consecutive weeks)
    • Ranked world number one again from June 2005 to October 2010 (281 consecutive weeks).
    • PGA Tour Player of The Year in 1997, 1999, 2000, 2001, 2002, 2003, 2005, 2006, 2007, 2009, 2013
    • Byron Nelson Award winner in 1999, 2000, 2001, 2002, 2003, 2005, 2006, 2007, 2009
    • PGA Tour Rookie of The Year in 1996
    • Inducted into the World Golf Hall of Fame in 2021
  2. Majors and Tournaments won
    • Masters in 1997, 2001, 2002, 2005, 2019
    • PGA Championship in 1999, 2000, 2006, 2007
    • U.S. Open in 2000, 2002, 2008
    • The Open Championship in 2000, 2005, 2006
    • 82 wins on the PGA Tour
    • 41 on the European Tour
    • 3 on the Japan Golf Tour
    • 2 on the Asian Tour
    • 3 on the Australian PGA Tour
    • 17 other
